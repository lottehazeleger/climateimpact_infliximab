Formulas used

Subcutaneous:
SC_GHG = 28.9 / 549 × dose_in_mg

Intravenous:
IV_GHG = 5.26 + 42.15 / 614 × dose_in_mg + 22.89 / 54.6 × 2 × distance_in_km

The travel distance is treated as a one-way distance and multiplied by 2 in the intravenous formula.
