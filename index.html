Formulas used

Subcutaneous:
SC_GHG = (28.9 / 549) × dosage × per_X_weeks

Intravenous:
IV_GHG = 5.26 + (42.15 / 614) × dose_mg_per_kg × patient_weight_kg × (per_X_weeks / 8) + (22.89 / (54.6 × 1.28)) × 2 × distance_km × (per_X_weeks / 8)

Results are expressed in kgCO2-eq.
