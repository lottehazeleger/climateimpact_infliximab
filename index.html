Formulas used

Subcutaneous: SC_GHG = (28.9 / 549) × dosage × (8 / SC_weeks)

Intravenous: IV_GHG = 5.26 + (42.15 / 614) × dose_mg_per_kg × patient_weight_kg × (8 / IV_weeks) + (22.89 / (54.6 × 1.28)) × 2 × distance_km × (8 / IV_weeks)

The interval is calculated as 8 / X weeks, so shorter intervals produce higher estimated emissions because there are more administrations within the same reference period.
